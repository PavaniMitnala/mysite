Why Apex Badminton Academy

Shuttler Sports Academy was established by Entrepreneur who is very passionate about Badminton with the aim of promoting badminton as fitness and fun activity in a club class environment. The Academy has quickly strengthened its presence in the field of Badminton by joining hands with some reputed coaches in Hyderabad.

The Academy provides world-class facilities with international standard synthetic courts, professional court lighting, and experienced coaches.

You can take membership in any of our centers across Hyderabad.
